SECTION III: ACCESSORIES OR OPTIONS

Oil Type Transformers Standard Features:

  • Hermetically sealed with corrugated tank design (for small and medium transformers)
  • Conservator type design (for 3000kVA and above)
  • Off circuit tap changer
  • Pressure relief valve
  • Drain valve
  • Lifting lugs
  • Earthing terminals
  • Open bushings for HV and LV sides

Dry Type Transformers Standard Features:

  • Temperature monitoring unit
  • 3 Nos. of pt100 probes
  • IP 21 rated enclosure

SECTION IV: TERMS AND CONDITIONS

The standard delivery time for transformers is 12-13 weeks. Please indicate below or reach out to our sales engineer if you require expedited delivery.

The standard warranty period is 2 years from delivery or 1.5 years from installation and commissioning, whichever comes first. Please specify if you need an extended warranty, which can be provided for an additional charge.

The following routine tests are conducted prior to the delivery of each oil type and dry type transformer:

  • Ratio & Vector Group Test
  • DC Resistance Test
  • No Load Test
  • Load Test
  • Separate Source Voltage Withstand Test
  • Induced Over Voltage Withstand Test
  • Insulation Resistance Test
  • Oil Dielectric Strength Test
  • Tank Pressure Test
