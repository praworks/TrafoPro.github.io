FAQ

Q: What is the purpose of this application?

A: This application is used to calculate the transformer specifications based on the user input.

Q: What is session-id?

A: Session-id is a unique identifier for each user session. It is used to identify the user session and store the user input data.

Q: How to get new session-id?

A: Click on the "Get new session-id" button to get new session-id.

Q: How to calculate the transformer specifications?

A: Fill in the customer name, country, contract number and transformer specifications. Click on the "Calculate" button to calculate the transformer specifications.

Q: How to download the transformer specifications in CSV format?

A: Click on the "Download CSV" button to download the transformer specifications in CSV format.
